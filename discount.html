Traveloka is here to give you everyday deals to maximize your trip. Whether on Traveloka Web or Traveloka App, you can always get Traveloka Coupons and use promo codes to purchase any Traveloka products and get discounts.

You can get information about the ongoing Traveloka promos by visiting the homepage of the Traveloka website and clicking the “% Deals” section. Meanwhile, on the Traveloka App, you can easily spot the “Promos” section on the homepage. Then, easily tap on the desired promo based on your needs and read the Terms & Conditions. There are multiple categories to choose from, from Flight promos, Hotel promos, Ground Transport promos, and many more. How do I find cheap deals in Traveloka?

Finding the best deals in Traveloka is supposed to be the easiest part of your pre-departure trip. You can always check the latest promo deals by visiting Traveloka's “Promo” page on Traveloka Web and Traveloka App to see all of today's promotions and offers on Hotels, Flights, Activities, and more.

Besides visiting the “Promo” page, finding cheap deals in the Traveloka App is recommended, as you can easily filter your search with the “Promo” filter to only sort Hotels or Activities with discounted prices or special deals. Specifically for Flight booking, there is a Price Alerts feature inside the Traveloka App that can help you get the best price on flight tickets.
